Through our grant review boards, we provide grants to area businesses, nonprofit organizations and local governments to improve energy efficiency; educate and retrain workers for the next generation of jobs; and fund energy technology projects that improve our environment.

Weatherization

We provide grants to energy efficiency and weatherization projects within Lewis County and South Thurston County, Washington.

ECONOMIC & COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT

We award grants to projects focused on education and retraining for workers, as well as economic development and community enhancement in Lewis County and South Thurston County, Washington.

ENERGY TECHNOLOGY

We supply grant awards to energy technology projects with the potential to create considerable clean energy, air quality, or other environmental benefits in Washington state.
